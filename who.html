About me

PROFESSIONAL PATH

I have graduated from Electrical and Electronics Department from Bilkent University in January 2016. Then, I have channeled my inner curiosity about human consciousness and brain processes into an academic goal.

My next step was to pursue a MSc degree in METU Cognitive Science Department. During my masters study my research aim was to investigate the own-age bias in facial age prediction, and integrate the so called bias into a neural network architecture and with this integration observe what does the deep-layers do to classify ages of face images. I have released the software part of my thesis work in a GitHub repository and you may find my Master of Science Thesis here.

As of Autumn 2019, I am a part of Dr. Gordon Feld's research group: Psychology & Neurobiology of Sleep & Memory at ZI Mannheim (Central Institute of Mental Health - CIMH) as a PhD researcher.
