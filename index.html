Inceptio




"Chaos, a raw and undivided mass,
Naught but lifeless bulk, with warring seeds
Of ill-joined elements compressed together."
Ovid, Methamorphoses, Book I, 1.9-11

As Ovid told, chaos existed before everything; just as a blob of tangled thoughts precede full-fledged arguments.

This website reflects my representations, my observations and my experiences regarding the entity we call 'life'.
